#### Using lasers to bend rigid materials

Kerf bending in wood, when not used in a laser context results in cuts that are like the one seen below, where the cut does not cut all the way across the material.

These kerf bends allow the material to partially bend in the direction that the cut occurs from, but more significantly allow the cut in the opposite direction.
